Our Story

Our Journey at Blujay

Blujay started with a simple belief: talent is everywhere, but opportunities are not. We began as a small training initiative helping students understand real industry skills.

Over time, Blujay grew into a structured IT training and placement platform focused on practical learning, hands-on projects, and career support. Our journey continues as we work to transform more lives through skill-based education.
