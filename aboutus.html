Who We Are

Building India's Tech Future

A comprehensive ecosystem combining technical education with professional digital services

Founded in 2020 by Cheruku Hari Krishna and Arjun Thumati, Blujay Technologies emerged with a clear vision: bridge the gap between academic learning and industry demands while creating meaningful employment opportunities across India.

From our strategic location in Ameerpet, Hyderabad's premier IT hub, we've successfully trained over 3,000 professionals through our unique dual-force model—combining cutting-edge technical training with professional digital services.

Our ambitious 1 Million Jobs mission drives everything we do, as we work to transform India's tech workforce with industry-ready skills and practical experience.
